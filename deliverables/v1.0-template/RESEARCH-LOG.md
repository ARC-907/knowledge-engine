# Research Log

> Session-level activity log for content/research changes inside `corpus/`. Buyer-maintained — append as you populate the corpus and as research activities occur.

## Starting state — three demonstration libraries

- The template ships with three curated demonstration libraries under
  `corpus/libraries/` (Decision Analysis, AI Monetization, System Design — 42
  markdown files) plus `corpus/samples/demo-library/` and
  `corpus/samples/demo-skill/`.
- The three libraries are *demonstration* material — they show the three-lens
  organizational methodology. Replace them with your own content; see
  `docs/BRING-YOUR-OWN-CORPUS.md`.
- This log resets per workspace; use it the same way you'd use a per-library
  `RESEARCH-LOG.md` — append an entry as you add, revise, or research content.
